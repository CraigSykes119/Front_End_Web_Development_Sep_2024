The Departed

The Departed (2006) is a crime thriller directed by Martin Scorsese. It follows two men on opposite sides of the law: an undercover cop infiltrating a criminal organization and a mole from that same gang working within the police force. As both try to uncover each other's identities, tensions rise, leading to a high-stakes game of deception and betrayal in Boston's criminal underworld.

Main Actors

  • Leonardo DiCaprio
  • Matt Damon
  • Jack Nicholson
  • Mark Wahlberg
