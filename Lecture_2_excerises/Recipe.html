My Favourite Recipe: Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted water according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, saut√© the chopped onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the minced beef to the pan and cook until browned all over.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
