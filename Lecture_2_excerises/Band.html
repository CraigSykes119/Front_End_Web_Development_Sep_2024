My Favourite Albums

Let the People Sing (1972)
This album is a powerful anthem of Irish nationalism, with iconic songs that capture the spirit of resistance and patriotism.
Irish to the Core (1987)
A mix of lively tunes and historical ballads, this album showcases the band's commitment to celebrating Irish identity and heritage.
Across the Broad Atlantic (1993)
Highlighting the Irish immigrant experience, this album offers a blend of traditional folk and emotional storytelling that resonates with themes of diaspora and longing.
